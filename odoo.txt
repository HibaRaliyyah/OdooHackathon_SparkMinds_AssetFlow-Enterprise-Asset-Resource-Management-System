### 🧪 Step-by-Step Testing Instructions

#### 1. Login & Dashboard Validation
1. Open your browser and navigate to **`http://localhost:5174`**.
2. You will be redirected to the `/login` page. Enter the **Admin** credentials above (or click the "Admin" button under Demo Accounts) and click **Sign In**.
3. You will be redirected to the **Dashboard**. 
4. **Verify:** Check that the KPI statistic cards (Total Assets, Available, Allocated, etc.) display correct numbers. Verify that the "Asset by Category" Pie chart and "Department Asset Usage" Bar chart are rendering correctly.

#### 2. Asset Management & QR Codes
1. Click on **Assets** in the left sidebar.
2. **Verify:** You should see a list of all seeded assets (e.g., MacBook Pro, Conference Room A).
3. Click on any asset in the list to open the newly built **Asset Details** page.
4. **Verify:** Ensure all details (Brand, Model, Warranty) are displayed. Look at the right sidebar to confirm the **QR Code** has rendered.
5. Click the **Print QR** button to verify that the browser print dialog opens correctly.

#### 3. Employee Profiles
1. Click on **Employees** in the left sidebar.
2. Click on the name of any employee (e.g., *Maya Patel*).
3. **Verify:** The **Employee Details** page should open, displaying their profile information, department, and a grid showing the specific assets (e.g., laptops, monitors) assigned to them.

#### 4. Audits Module
1. Click on **Audits** in the left sidebar.
2. **Verify:** The Audits dashboard should load. Since we haven't seeded any audits yet, it will display a modern empty state ("No audits scheduled"). Click the **Schedule Audit** button to verify the UI interaction.

#### 5. Additional Modules
Feel free to click through the other modules to verify the complete UI:
*   **Maintenance:** View pending and in-progress repair tickets.
*   **Transfers:** Check out the asset transfer request interface.
*   **AI Insights:** Navigate to the AI dashboard (sparkles icon) to view the natural language query interface.
*   **Settings:** Verify the multi-tab settings interface (Profile, Security, Appearance).
